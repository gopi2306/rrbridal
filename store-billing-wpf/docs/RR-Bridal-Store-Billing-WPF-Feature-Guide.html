RR Bridal Store Billing (WPF) - End-to-End Feature Guide

Document version: 1.0  |  Generated: 19 June 2026
Application: RRBridal.StoreBilling.App (TruBill POS)
Purpose: Offline-first retail POS for RR Bridal stores with central sync

1. Application Overview

The WPF Store Billing application is the point-of-sale client for RR Bridal retail counters. Bills, returns, and day-close data are saved to local MongoDB and pushed to the central NestJS API when connectivity is available.

  • Each till uses unique DEVICE_ID and POS_COUNTER in the .env file beside the executable.
  • POS Counter 1 is the primary (manager) till with full navigation and auto-sync.
  • Secondary counters can bill, return, close day, and print barcodes but cannot open Settings, Dashboard, Ledger, Analytics, Online Sales, or Expenses.
  • Product master, stock, promotion schemes, and users are pulled from central; sales events are pushed via an outbox queue.

2. Login, Session and Security

2.1 Cashier login (local)

  • Email + password against store_users in local MongoDB (synced from central at startup).
  • BCrypt verification; one active session per user per DEVICE_ID (machine lock).
  • Logout via header icon or Navigation menu; returns to login screen.

2.2 Central API login (Settings)

  • Used for sync, receipt profile, WhatsApp API, and company profile.
  • Settings > Connection & sync: email/password login; token in central_auth.json.

2.3 Per-user limits

  • maxDiscountPercent caps combined item % and cash discount at bill post.
  • Till number (POS_COUNTER) controls navigation access, not user role field.

Header: logo, store name, page title, global search, day status, user badge, notifications, settings, logout. Left hamburger menu opens full navigation. Footer shows keyboard shortcuts.

4. Billing (POS)

4.1 Customer section

  • Fields: customer code, phone (10-digit required), name, salesman (read-only).
  • Find Customer dialog; phone auto-search; quick capture for walk-ins.
  • Navigate to Customers to register full profile and apply to bill.

4.2 Bill flags

  • Hold bills, Door delivery, Online COD, Stitching (+ delivery date), Print invoice.

4.3 Line items

  • Entry row: SKU/barcode + Enter adds line; name opens product pick list.
  • Lookup from local product cache; Add manual line; Import CSV (stub).
  • Configurable columns: Minimal / Standard / Full (Settings > Other).
  • Stock validation at post; stock decremented on successful post.

4.4 Discounts and totals

  • Item discount %, cash discount, promotion schemes, round off, inter-state IGST toggle.
  • Apply customer credit notes; Online COD cannot combine with credit note.

4.5 Hold, post, and payment

  • F8 Hold - save draft with hold number.
  • F9 Post - requires name, phone, lines, open business day; assigns bill number.
  • Payment dialog: Cash, Card (Pine Labs), UPI (Razorpay), Credit note, Split, Online COD (no payment at post).
  • Optional WhatsApp auto-send and print after post.

5. Customer Registration

  • Auto-generated customer code; saves locally and syncs to central API.
  • Required: name, mobile. Optional: address, GSTIN, email, credit customer flag.
  • F4 Save, Escape Cancel on Customers page.

6. Sale Returns

  • Lookup bill by invoice number or last 3-4 digits.
  • Return mode: credit note or cash refund.
  • Exchange lines with payment for difference; stock updated; thermal receipt for exchange.

7. Adjustments

  • Load posted bill; edit adjusted qty/rate; reason required; day must be open.

8. Duplicate Print

  • Bill tab: search and reprint with DUPLICATE watermark; Send WhatsApp.
  • Credit Note tab: search and reprint linked sale-return receipt.
  • F11 shortcut; can be disabled in Settings.

9. Day Close and Cash Management

  • Open day with opening cash; live summary of bills, tender, expenses, movements.
  • Cash deposit and withdrawal entries.
  • Cash hand-over with denomination counting; close day.
  • Download full report (CSV or Excel).
  • Daily expenses (primary till only).

10. Dashboard and Analytics

  • Dashboard: metrics, POS filter, day-close panel, stock exceptions, inventory grid, bill list with export.
  • Analytics: 14-day daily sales trend.

11. Online Sales (primary till)

  • Track Online COD orders; record payment with mode and transaction number.
  • Balance till summary for pending COD receivables.

12. Ledger (primary till)

  • Recent bills and payments; duplicate reprint for selected bill.

13. Barcode Label Printing

  • Add products by SKU/barcode; F6 pick list; set print qty per line.
  • F5 print labels (TSPL/ZPL); F7 clear screen.

14. Settings (4 tabs)

Connection and sync

  • Central login/logout; Run sync; view outbox count, cursor, diagnostics, auto-sync status.

Receipt and printing

  • Store header fields; thermal and office printers; Thermal / A4 / A5 format.
  • A5 pre-printed alignment editor; also print thermal first option.

WhatsApp

  • Auto-send bill on post; connection status; test send.

Other

  • Allow duplicate print; CN remaining cash payout; line item column detail level.
  • Developer test buttons: Pine Labs, Razorpay, Purchase intent.

15. Sync with Central Backend

  • Push outbox events (bills, returns, expenses, day sessions, COD payments, etc.).
  • Pull products, stock transfers, promotion schemes, masters, store users.
  • Periodic auto-sync on primary till when SYNC_INTERVAL_MINUTES > 0.

18. Typical Daily Workflow

  1. Login with cashier credentials.
  2. Primary till: Settings - Login to central - Run sync.
  3. Day Close - Open day with opening cash.
  4. Billing - customer phone - add items - discounts - F9 Post - Payment.
  5. Print / WhatsApp as configured.
  6. Process returns or adjustments as needed.
  7. Record expenses and cash movements (primary till).
  8. Day Close - Cash hand-over - Close day.
  9. Download day-close report.
  10. Logout; auto-sync pushes events to central.

20. Known Limitations

  • Import CSV on Billing is not yet implemented.
  • Exchange credit-note duplicate reprint has limited support.
  • User role field is synced but not enforced in UI.
